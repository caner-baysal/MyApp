# Release Checklist

- [ ] Test auth flow on a real development build.
- [ ] Test renewal notifications on a development build.
- [ ] Review Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
- [ ] Confirm backend production URL is configured.
- [ ] Disable or review remote brand icon usage (`iconUrl`) before App Store / Google Play submission.
- [ ] Ensure App Store screenshots do not show third-party brand logos unless cleared for use.
